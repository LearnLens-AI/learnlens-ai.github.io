About AI Intern

AI Intern is an AI-powered educational assistant developed by LearnLens AI. The platform combines Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), document analysis, and personalized learning to help engineering students learn faster and better.

Features

Engineering Tutor

Explain complex concepts in simple and structured ways.

PDF Analysis

Upload notes and research papers for intelligent insights.

RAG Search

Accurate answers using domain-specific knowledge retrieval.

Personalized Learning

Adaptive explanations based on student skill level.

Project Guidance

Support for projects, internships, and technical growth.

Future AI Mentor

Moving toward a complete engineering learning companion.
